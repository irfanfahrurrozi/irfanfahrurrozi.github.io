Who Am I?

Hi I'm Irfan Fahrurrozi. I love to help anyone having their own website who very short on money. So, I created their low-cost website with powerful resources beyond what they expected.

Im also doing some designs (specially on character design) through my passion in art. Sometimes reading books, watching Astro Physics video on youtube, and absolutely doing something great in my life.

I was working for Twitter Indonesia as Twitter translator in Twitter Translation Center ( TTC ) community. Starting in 2013, I already translated many Twitter string datas from English phrase to Indonesian phrase. On November 2017, the community were closed by Twitter, Inc. as an impact to their new policy to using a translation company to help them localising the whole Twitter environment afterward.
